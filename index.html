Our story

A bakery built around people.

Our West LA kitchen is rooted in European baking traditions with a Levantine twist. Fresh breads, classic pastries, and seasonal offerings made with care — and a mission that goes beyond the bread.

A tribute.

Louma Bakery is named after my sister and a tribute to the warmth of her presence. A friend once said, "Being around Louma is like pulling fresh cookies from the oven." She teaches us to savor the little moments — comfort, connection, and community.

The kitchen.

Our West LA kitchen is rooted in European baking traditions with a Levantine twist. We serve fresh breads, classic pastries, and seasonal offerings — think organic seeded sourdough meets our house-made fattoush salad.

The purpose.

Our mission is to create supportive training and employment opportunities for individuals with learning and developmental differences. We believe that everyone deserves an opportunity to grow, contribute, and cultivate their passion. All profits go directly to The Louma Initiative, a 501(c)(3) public charity.

Our mission

Bread, pastry, and opportunity.

Our mission is to create supportive training and employment opportunities for individuals with learning and developmental differences in a professional bakery setting. Grounded in Louma's own experience, we believe that everyone deserves an opportunity to grow, contribute, and cultivate their passion.

Who we are

All profits from the bakery go directly to The Louma Initiative, a 501(c)(3) public charity that fosters meaningful opportunities for people with intellectual and developmental differences.

What we believe

Everyone deserves an opportunity to grow, contribute, and cultivate their passion. This is not charity. This is real work, real craft, and real careers — in a professional bakery setting built on patience and respect.

How it works

Every team member receives hands-on training and mentorship in our West LA kitchen. We are building careers alongside beautiful bread — one croissant, one sourdough, one morning at a time.

How you help

When you buy a pastry, a loaf, or a box — you are directly funding The Louma Initiative. Every visit matters. Every order contributes.
