Our story

A bakery built around people.

Our West LA kitchen is rooted in European baking traditions with a Levantine twist.

A tribute.

Louma Bakery is named after my sister and a tribute to the warmth of her presence. A friend once said, "Being around Louma is like pulling fresh cookies from the oven."

The kitchen.

Our West LA kitchen is rooted in European baking traditions with a Levantine twist. Think organic seeded sourdough meets our house-made fattoush salad.

The purpose.

All profits go directly to The Louma Initiative, a 501(c)(3) public charity supporting employment for people with learning and developmental differences.

Our mission

Bread, pastry, and opportunity.

Supportive training and employment for people with learning and developmental differences, in a professional bakery setting.

Who we are

All profits from the bakery go directly to The Louma Initiative, a 501(c)(3) public charity that fosters meaningful opportunities for people with intellectual and developmental differences.

What we believe

Everyone deserves an opportunity to grow, contribute, and cultivate their passion. This is real work, real craft, and real careers in a professional bakery setting built on patience and respect.

How it works

Every team member receives hands-on training and mentorship in our West LA kitchen. We are building careers alongside beautiful bread.

How you help

When you buy a pastry, a loaf, or a box, you are directly funding The Louma Initiative.
