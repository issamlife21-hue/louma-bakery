Our story

A bakery built around people.

Louma is a Mediterranean-inspired bakery and coffee bar in the heart of Santa Monica — a small, bright space designed for slow mornings, quick stops, and good conversation.

Louma began with a simple idea: that the food we love most often comes from the places we know best. Our menu draws from Mediterranean and Levantine traditions — za'atar, labneh, pistachio, rose, orange blossom, tahini — woven into the French pastry techniques we trained in.

We bake every morning from scratch. Doughs are slow-fermented, fillings are made in-house, and our coffee is pulled with the same care we put into a croissant. The goal isn't to be fancy. It's to be genuinely good, every day.

Louma is part of The Louma Initiative, a nonprofit committed to inclusive employment — creating dignified work for people who are too often left out of the workforce. When you buy a pastry here, you're helping fund training, mentorship, and real careers for our team.

Our mission

Bread, pastry, and opportunity.

Every person deserves meaningful work. At Louma, we believe a bakery can be both a great place to eat and a place that genuinely changes lives.

What We Believe

Every purchase at Louma supports real employment, real training, and real careers for people with learning and developmental differences.

Who We Support

Louma is structured to create professional opportunities for individuals with learning and developmental differences. This is not charity. This is employment.

How It Works

Every team member receives hands-on training, mentorship, and a supportive environment built on patience, respect, and collaboration. We are building careers alongside beautiful bread.

How You Help

When you buy a croissant, a loaf, or a pastry box, you are contributing to something larger than breakfast. Every visit matters.
