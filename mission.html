Why we exist

Mission

A bakery, and a workforce-development initiative.

The Louma Initiative trains and employs people with intellectual and developmental differences as bakers, baristas, and front-of-house teammates. They work the line in plain view, not in a separate program behind a door. Every dollar the bakery makes funds wages, training, and a real path to a career in food.

Each teammate trains alongside our head bakers and baristas. They learn lamination, espresso, customer service, and how to run a station from open to close.

We hire to keep. Full-time hours, real wages, benefits, and a job that gets harder and more interesting over time.
